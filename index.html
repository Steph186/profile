Resume

May 2018 – June 2018

9-week FullStack Bootcamp in web development at Le Wagon Milano, Italy.

Intensive coding bootcamp learning HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, SQL, git & GitHub, Heroku and Ruby on Rails. Designed, Implemented and Shipped to production a clone of AirBnBa clone of AirBnB and a Rails prototype of Coalla.me.

Dec 2017 – Feb 2018

Machine Learning & Deep Learning Specialization, Andrew NG, Coursera.

Neural Networks and Deep Learning, Improving Deep Neural Networks, Structuring Machine Learning Projects, Convolutional Neural Networks, Sequence Models.

See certificates

2015-2017

Master in Cognitive Neuroscience, Cum Laude, CIMeC – Center of Mind and Brain, University of Trento, Italy.

Internship and Master Thesis: fMRI study about visual & auditory motion perception

April 2016 - Octobre 2017

CIMeC – Center of Mind June 17 and Brain, University of Trento, Italy, Advisor: Prof. O. Collignon.

The aim of the project was to understand whether the motion area of the brain processes information independently of how it senses motion. We used support vector machine to differentiate (classify) the direction of the motion perceived by the subject, based on his neural activity.

Presentation of the master thesis in conferences

  • Ten years of Mind/Brain Sciences - 19th-21th October, Rovereto, Italy
  • Workshop on Concepts, Action and Objects (CAOs) - 4th-7th May, Rovereto, Italy

2012-2015

Bachelor in Psychology & Educational Sciences, Cum Laude, UCL – Université Catholique de Louvain, Belgium.

Second prize of the jury for the integrative work of the Bachelor Degree.
